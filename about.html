About Avocados Always Fresh

Avocados Always Fresh is a locally owned restaurant in Pocasset, Massachusetts serving fresh breakfast, lunch, seafood, and homestyle meals. Our goal is to provide delicious food in a friendly and welcoming environment for families, workers, and visitors to Cape Cod.
